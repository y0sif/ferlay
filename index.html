Why Ferlay

Built for developers

Remote Control

Start and manage Claude Code sessions from your phone. No SSH, no VPN, no port forwarding.

End-to-End Encrypted

X25519 key exchange + AES-256-GCM. The relay server never sees your data.

Cross-Platform

Daemon runs on Linux, macOS, and Windows. App available for Android and iOS.

Architecture

How it works

1

Install the daemon

One command sets up the daemon on your machine and starts it as a background service.

2

Scan QR to pair

Open the Ferlay app on your phone and scan the QR code. Keys are exchanged, encryption is established.

3

Control from anywhere

Start sessions, approve actions, and manage your AI agent. All traffic is encrypted end-to-end.
